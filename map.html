The land of Kemet

Ancient Egyptian Cities

"Journey through the sacred lands of ancient Egypt — from the golden plateau of Giza to the holy city of Abydos. Six iconic cities, each holding its own story, its own gods, and its own eternal monuments. Click on any location to discover the wonders that still stand today."

GIZA

Home to the last surviving Wonder of the Ancient World — the Great Pyramid of Khufu stands eternal on the Giza Plateau.

POPULAR PLACES

🔺 The Great Pyramid 🦁 The Great Sphinx ⛩️ Solar Boat Museum

LUXOR

Once the ancient capital Thebes — the world's greatest open-air museum, stretching along both banks of the Nile.

POPULAR PLACES

🏛️ Karnak Temple ⚰️ Valley of the Kings 🌅 Luxor Temple

ALEXANDRIA

The jewel of the Mediterranean — founded by Alexander the Great and home to one of the greatest libraries ever built.

POPULAR PLACES

💡 The Great Lighthouse 📚 Library of Alexandria 🏰 Qaitbay Citadel

MEMPHIS

The first capital of unified Egypt — founded by Pharaoh Menes and sacred to Ptah, the god of craftsmen and architects.

POPULAR PLACES

🏛️ Temple of Ptah 🔺 Step Pyramid of Saqqara 🗿 Colossus of Ramesses II

ASWAN

The gateway to Africa — a city of golden sands, blue waters, and temples carved directly into the mountainside.

POPULAR PLACES

🗿 Abu Simbel Temples 🏛️ Philae Temple 🌊 Aswan High Dam

ABYDOS

The holiest city in ancient Egypt — the eternal resting place of Osiris and home to the oldest known royal tombs.

POPULAR PLACES

⚱️ Temple of Seti I 🌊 The Osireion 📜 Abydos King List
